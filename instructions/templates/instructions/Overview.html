You will be playing a virtual dice rolling game.

The task consists of shaking your phone to roll the virtual dice. Each round you will be given a target number from 1 to 6. If the dice you roll lands on your target number, you will receive {{Constants.round_payoff}} for that round.

Your shaking will be recorded from the accelerometer on your phone. After 5 seconds of shaking, the game will automatically pause, and a button will appear. When you press this button, you will see the dice released and rolled onto the dice table with a speed proportional to how hard you shook your phone.
