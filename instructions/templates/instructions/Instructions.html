The task begins with a loading screen that displays your target for the round.

Once you click on the "begin" button, the trial will begin.

The 5 second timer will begin as soon as you click the button. During this time you will see a 3D scene showing your dice (purple) in the dice cup (blue) through a semi-opaque lid. As you shake your phone, energy from your shake will be transferred to the dice and you will see and hear it rattle around the dice cup.

You will also see a reminder of your target in the top left of the screen and your accumulated winnings so far in the top right.

To indicate the passage of the 5 seconds, there will be 3 audible bleeps - two short and one long. Once the long bleep is sounds, the 5 seconds has elapsed and the simulation will be suspended. No further shaking after the long bleep will affect the dice on this trial. A button will appear on your screen to continue.

When you are ready to view the results of your roll, you can click the button to view the results of your roll.

If the die lands with your target number face up, you will receive {{Constants.round_payoff}} added to your total bonus.
